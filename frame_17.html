The team

With over 20 years manufacturing experience, our team of skilled craftsmen, technical designers and furniture professionals are the very best in their sector, producing luxury furniture for leading interior professionals and Trade clients worldwide.

From our London Workshops, we apply the same bespoke approach to each product, creating exceptional handcrafted luxury upholstery and high-end furniture. Our multi-skilled team are well versed combing wood, metal, stone with specialist metal and veneer finishes for both modern and traditional products.
About the Founder

Our workshops are home to a team of multi skilled professionals creating luxury furniture in a variety of materials, including wood, stone, metal and specialist finishes.

We are experienced in all aspects of bespoke luxury furniture, from large scale bespoke joinery and custom designs through to intricate metal works and stone. Each product is perfectly tailored to your exact requirements, nurturing every step of the handcrafted process to take your design to a finished installation.
We are manufacturers

The first stage of any order starts with our furniture design team. Our collaborative approach with clients and decades of experience enable us to produce samples and prototypes in exceptionally short leadtimes as working models for workshop visits or site presentation.

The design department provides concept designs, including line drawings, detailed CAD drawings and CGI for client approval. Quality and build is carefully considered in all details and materials we use
The workshop

Ateliers London completes a comprehensive process of quality control prior to any goods being delivered to site or packed for International transport.

Our team will often inspect the site for any access and fit-out requirements, ensuring the perfect outcome is achieved. The Project Team oversee the entire process to ensure each order is completed on time, directing the Transport and Installation team to provide a full white glove installation regardless of the complexity
