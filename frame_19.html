Prototypes & Samples

The first stage of any order starts with our furniture design team. Our collaborative approach with clients and decades of experience enable us to produce samples and prototypes in exceptionally short leadtimes as working models for workshop visits or site presentation.

The design department provides concept designs, including line drawings, detailed CAD drawings and CGI for client approval. Quality and build is carefully considered in all details and materials we use
Delivery & Installation

Ateliers London completes a comprehensive process of quality control prior to any goods being delivered to site or packed for International transport.

Our team will often inspect the site for any access and fit-out requirements, ensuring the perfect outcome is achieved. The Project Team oversee the entire process to ensure each order is completed on time, directing the Transport and Installation team to provide a full white glove installation regardless of the complexity
